## Core Features & Benefits

### 1. Hero Creative
**Feature:** Automatically highlights your best-performing creative
**Benefit:** Scale what works, pause what doesn't—all in one glance
**Use Case:** Quick decision-making on budget allocation

### 2. Top Performing Creatives
**Feature:** All creatives sorted by performance metrics
**Benefit:** One-click sorting by leads, CPL, CTR, or any metric that matters
**Use Case:** Identify winners and losers instantly

### 3. Share of Creatives
**Feature:** Visual breakdown of ad spend distribution
**Benefit:** See exactly where your ad spend goes; identify over/under-invested creative types
**Use Case:** Budget optimization and reallocation

### 4. Creative Profile
**Feature:** Detailed performance view for each ad
**Benefit:** Full story with performance trends, audience data, and fatigue indicators in one view
**Use Case:** Deep-dive analysis for strategic decisions

### 5. Marketing Funnel
**Feature:** Track audience journey from impression to lead
**Benefit:** See exactly where people engage and where they drop off
**Use Case:** Funnel optimization and conversion improvement

### 6. Team View
**Feature:** Collaborative workspace for teams and freelancers
**Benefit:** Everyone sees the same real-time data; no more exporting reports or sharing screens
**Use Case:** Seamless team collaboration
